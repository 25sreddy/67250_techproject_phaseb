Welcome to MonoMuse

Museum overview

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum where art and technology meet.

MonoMuse serves as a modern hub where the worlds of artistic expression and digital advancement collide. By blending immersive technology with traditional creativity, the museum provides a unique space for guests to engage with evolving cultural landscapes through tactile displays and ever-changing galleries.

Informed by our ticket funnel analysis

Phase A data on MonoMuse’s old site showed visitors took about 10 page views per purchase—roughly double a typical checkout flow—and many sessions that clicked Buy Tickets never finished, pointing to a long path and frustrating checkout.

This redesign responds directly: Buy Tickets stays in the navigation on every page, pricing and checkout live on one tickets page, and the form uses clear labels, live pricing, and inline errors so you are less likely to lose your place before confirming your order.
