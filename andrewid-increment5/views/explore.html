About the museum

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum focused on the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity.

Analytics also suggested visitors were browsing many informational pages before they could even start a ticket purchase. On this site, hours and directions live here on Explore alongside the map and video, while Buy Tickets remains one click away in the nav from every page—so planning and purchasing are no longer at odds.

  • Mission: Inspire curiosity through art + technology experiences.
  • Who we serve: Students, families, artists, technologists, and the wider community.
  • Founded: 2016
  • Milestones: First interactive gallery (2018), annual digital arts festival (2021), rotating AI art showcase (2024).
