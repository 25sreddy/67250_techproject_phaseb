Tickets & visit planning

Plan your visit: review prices below, then complete checkout. We are open Mon–Sun, 10am–8pm.

Our Data Analysis Report found that many users who clicked Buy Tickets still abandoned checkout, and that the old flow required far too many steps across unrelated pages before payment.

This page keeps prices, schedule context, and checkout together, shows your estimated total as you choose type and quantity, and sends you to a confirmation screen after Place Order so the path from intent to “done” is short and predictable.

Time-of-day analysis in the same report pointed to midday and afternoon as when visitors were most likely to follow through—we are open 10am–8pm daily so you can align a real visit with those high-intent windows.
