MonoMuse is a contemporary museum focused on the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity.

  • Mission: Inspire curiosity through art + technology experiences.
  • Who we serve: Students, families, artists, technologists, and the wider community.
  • Founded: 2016
  • Milestones: First interactive gallery (2018), annual digital arts festival (2021), rotating AI art showcase (2024).
